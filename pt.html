❓ FAQ

Perguntas Frequentes

Can individual developers also make requests?
+
Absolutely. Studio EIM is open to everyone from major publishers to indie developers, regardless of project size. We promise the same quality and dedication for small projects. Feel free to contact us at contact@eim.kr.
Can you work on crowdfunded projects?
+
Welcome. We actively support crowdfunded games with flexible production plans tailored to your budget and schedule. Full-scale work can begin after successful funding.
Our budget is limited. Is full voice production possible?
+
Absolutely possible. We have numerous talented voice actors with attractive vocal qualities. We design optimal casting and recording plans for your budget. We never compromise on quality.
I'm worried that a small project might be treated with less care.
+
You can set those worries aside. Studio EIM has given its best to projects large and small for 26 years. Smaller projects offer closer communication and more detailed direction.
Do you also provide game music licensing services?
+
Yes, we provide one-stop support from copyright registration to music distribution. Game OST streaming, copyright management, and licensing — we handle the entire music business.
